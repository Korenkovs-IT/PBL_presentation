The Real Business Problem

Retail coffee shops face a serious people problem: high employee and manager turnover. Starbucks became a strong model because it organizes people well. If a coffee start-up wants stability, it must build a people system, not just a product.

Slide 11

Review & Empowerment

Review is continuous, not occasional. When review and empowerment happen together, employees don't just follow the system — they help improve it.

🔄
Three Review Actions
Gather employee feedback. Check whether service and staffing goals are met. Adjust training, schedules, or processes when results fall short.
💪
Four Empowerment Commitments
Share useful information. Involve employees in decisions. Give training and tools. Recognize contribution.
